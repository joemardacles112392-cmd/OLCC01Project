    Strategy

    In Dota 2, each team role from Position 1 to 5 has a distinct strategic purpose. Position 1 (Carry) focuses on farming early to become the late-game damage dealer who secures victory in team fights. Position 2 (Mid) controls tempo, often rotating to other lanes to pressure enemies and create space. Position 3 (Offlane) disrupts the enemy carry, builds durable items, and initiates fights to protect their own team. Position 4 (Soft Support) roams, provides vision, and sets up plays with disables or utility. Finally, Position 5 (Hard Support) secures the lane for the carry, buys wards, and sacrifices farm to ensure the team’s overall success.
