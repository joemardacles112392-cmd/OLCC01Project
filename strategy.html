    Maps

    The Dota 2 map is a large battlefield divided into Radiant and Dire sides, each with unique terrain advantages. It features three lanes—top, middle, and bottom—where creeps spawn and heroes clash to push toward enemy structures. Between the lanes lies the jungle, filled with neutral camps that provide gold, experience, and strategic control points. The map also includes key objectives like Roshan’s pit, runes, and towers that shape the flow of the game. Vision, warding, and controlling high ground areas are crucial, as the map’s layout heavily influences team strategy and positioning.
