    Items

    Dota 2 items are powerful tools that heroes use to strengthen their abilities and adapt to different situations in the game. They can provide bonuses such as increased damage, armor, health, mana, attack speed, or special active skills that can change the outcome of battles. Some items are basic and inexpensive, while others require combining multiple components to create stronger and more advanced equipment. Strategic item selection is important because the right build can counter enemy heroes and support your team’s strategy. Mastering item builds in Dota 2 helps players improve their performance and increases their chances of winning matches.
