    Armory

    Dota 2 offers a huge variety of armor and skin sets that let players customize their heroes with unique looks. Each set often reflects the hero’s lore, ranging from mystical robes to battle-forged armor. Some sets are rare and highly sought after, especially those released during special events or tournaments. Collecting and equipping these skins adds both style and personality to the gameplay experience.
