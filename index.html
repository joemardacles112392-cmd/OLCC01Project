Heroes, Abilities and Items



Anti-Mage

A highly mobile agility carry in Dota 2 who specializes in hunting down spellcasters and draining their mana. With abilities like Blink and Mana Break, he can quickly chase enemies while burning their mana with every attack. Anti-Mage becomes extremely powerful in the late game, using Mana Void to deal massive damage based on his enemies’ missing mana.


Abilities:

  • Mana Break
  • Blink
  • Counterspell
  • Mana Void (Ultimate)

Core Items:

  • Battle Fury
  • Manta Style
  • Black King Bar


Death Prophet


An intelligence hero in Dota 2 known for her powerful spirit-based abilities and strong pushing potential. She uses Crypt Swarm and Silence to damage and control enemies during team fights. Her ultimate, Exorcism, summons vengeful spirits that deal massive damage to heroes and buildings, making her a dominant force in mid-game battles.


Abilities:

  • Crypt Swarm
  • Silence
  • Spirit Siphon
  • Exorcism (Ultimate)

Core Items:

  • Eul's Scepter of Divinity
  • Shiva's Guard
  • Black King Bar


Pudge


A strength hero in Dota 2 famous for his ability to control the battlefield with his Meat Hook and Rot. He excels at catching enemies off guard, pulling them into deadly situations with precise positioning. Pudge’s ultimate, Dismember, immobilizes and damages a target, making him a terrifying threat in both solo kills and team fights.


< <

Abilities:

  • Meat Hook
  • Rot
  • Flesh Heap
  • Dismember (Ultimate)

Core Items:

  • Blink Dagger
  • Aether Lens
  • Aghanim's Scepter


Faceless Void


An agility hero in Dota 2 who manipulates time to gain an advantage in battles. His Time Walk allows him to rewind damage taken, while Time Lock gives him a chance to stun enemies with each attack. His ultimate, Chronosphere, freezes all units in a large area, enabling devastating team fights when timed correctly.

Abilities:

  • Time Walk
  • Time Dilation
  • Time Lock
  • Chronosphere (Ultimate)

Core Items:

  • Mask of Madness
  • Black King Bar
  • Mjollnir

Night Stalker


A strength hero in Dota 2 known for his dominance during nighttime. He thrives in darkness, gaining increased movement speed, attack speed, and vision control. Night Stalker’s abilities allow him to silence enemies, chase down fleeing targets, and turn the tide of battles when night falls.


Abilities:

  • Void
  • Crippling Fear
  • Hunter in the Night
  • Dark Ascension (Ultimate)

Core Items:

  • Black King Bar
  • Aghanim's Scepter
  • Assault Cuirass

Abaddon


Abaddon is a durable, melee strength hero in Dota 2, frequently played as a support or utility offlaner. He specializes in shielding teammates and sustaining himself through damage. His abilities Mist Coil and Aphotic Shield offer low cooldown healing and damage absorption, making him excellent at keeping allies alive during intense fights. His ultimate, Borrowed Time, acts as a panic button that converts incoming damage into healing, allowing him to dive deep into enemy territory.


Abilities:

  • Mist Coil
  • Aphotic Shield
  • Curse of Avernus
  • Borrowed Time (Ultimate)

Core Items:

  • Aghanim's Scepter
  • Harpoon
  • Radiance


Invoker


A versatile intelligence hero in Dota 2 who commands the elements through his unique ability to invoke spells. By combining Quas, Wex, and Exort, Invoker can unleash a wide array of powerful abilities, making him one of the most complex and rewarding heroes to master.

Abilities:

  • Quas
  • Wex
  • Exort
  • Invoke (Ultimate)

Core Items:

  • Aghanim's Scepter
  • Black King Bar
  • Scythe of Vyse

Zeus


Zeus is a powerful intelligence hero in Dota 2, known as the Lord of Olympus. He excels at dealing massive magical damage from a distance, punishing enemies with his lightning-based abilities. His global ultimate, Thundergod's Wrath, allows him to strike all enemy heroes no matter where they are on the map, making him a constant threat throughout the game.


Abilities:

  • Arc Lightning
  • Lightning Bolt
  • Static Field
  • Thundergod's Wrath (Ultimate)

Core Items:

  • Aether Lens
  • Veil of Discord
  • Refresher Orb

Leshrac


Leshrac is a ranged intelligence hero in Dota 2, known as the Tormented Soul. He excels at dealing continuous magical and mixed damage through his powerful area-of-effect abilities. With strong pushing potential and devastating team fight presence, Leshrac can quickly overwhelm enemies when supported by mana-sustaining items.


Abilities:

  • Split Earth
  • Diabolic Edict
  • Lightning Storm
  • Pulse Nova (Ultimate)

Core Items:

  • Bloodstone
  • Black King Bar
  • Boots of Travel
